You need to upload proof of address

We need proof that you live at this address.

You must upload a document with your name and address on it.

If you live in a care home

Residents must upload an invoice for your care home fees that includes:

  • the name and address of the care home
  • your name
  • December 2022 fees

All other applicants

You could upload a:

  • valid UK driving licence (provisional driving licences are not valid)
  • utility bill from the past 3 months (mobile phone bills and credit card statements are not valid)
  • bank statement from the past 3 months
  • tenancy agreement
  • council tax exemption certificate
  • signed, dated letter with a letterhead from a boat mooring provider that has your name and address and says you live permanently on a boat on a long-term home mooring

Your document must be:

  • no more than 10 MB
  • in PDF, .jpeg, .png or .doc format
